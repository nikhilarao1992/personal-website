About Me

My name is Nikhil Rao, and I'm a Predictive Data Analyst at Genentech in South San Francisco, CA. Through my undergraduate and graduate level education at the University of California, Berkeley, I became deeply involved in predictive modeling, visualization, and machine learning. Across my various projects, I've extensively used analytic techniques to predict patient enrollment among Phase I and II Clinical Trials, to forecast patient availability for studies in the Cancer Immunotherapy Network, and to conduct thorough competitive landscape analysis in the oncology sphere. With over 5 years of experience in data analytics and data science, I am enthusiastic about any project that involves a lot of data.

Prior to Genentech, I was a lecturer at U.C. Berkeley in the Economics Department, where I taught over hundreds of students for more than 2 years. I developed an immense passion for teaching and public speaking, and my dedication to academic instruction led me to winning the 2016 Outstanding Graduate Student Instructor award.

When I'm not typing away on the computer, I enjoy exploring new eateries and beautiful sceneries, watching exciting shows on Netflix, and lifting weights at the gym.
